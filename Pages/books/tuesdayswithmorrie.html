Tuesdays with morrie

Tuesdays with morrie was the very first book I ever read outside of my studies. It was recomended by a friend who to this day holds a special place in my heart. I downloaded the book on my phone and read it on an 8 hour trip to Limpopo from Johannesburg. This book changed my whole life. I transformed from an almost robotic like someone and trully came to know what it means to be a human being. It made me more human.

Lessons from the book Tuesdays with morrie by Mitch Albom

  • Life is a series of back & forth.
  • So many people walk around with a meaningless life.
  • The most important thing in life is to learn how to give out love, and how to let it come in.
  • Do the kind of things that come from the heart.
